Aaron Tripp

GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Aaron Tripp is a Vancouver-based graphic designer who specializes in film posters and advertisements. He is currently studying Digital Design and Development at the British Columbia Institute of Technology and is expected to graduate in 2024. Some of his interests include digital art, photography, filmmaking, acting, singing and playing guitar.
